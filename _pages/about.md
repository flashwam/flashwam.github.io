I am a second-year PhD candidate in Computer Engineering at [Northeastern University](https://www.northeastern.edu), co-advised by [Prof. Yanzhi Wang](https://coe.northeastern.edu/people/wang-yanzhi/) and [Prof. Xuan Zhang](https://coe.northeastern.edu/people/zhang-xuan-silvia/). Before joining Northeastern, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from the [University of Tehran](https://ut.ac.ir/en).

My research spans two directions:
- **Physical AI, World Models, and World-Action Models** — making these models efficient enough for real-time, real-world deployment.
- **Privacy-Preserving and Trustworthy Machine Learning** — approaching trustworthy AI through the lens of efficiency and efficient AI.

Feel free to reach out via email if you'd like to discuss research or potential collaborations.
